Studio Buddy

Studio Buddy is an Android and iOS compatible mobile app developed by Kennesaw State University (KSU) students in Fall 2022, in collaboration with the KSU Bailey School of Music. Currently, the app allows for music teachers to collaborate with their students by assigning individual and group practice plans, as well as providing resources to assist in music practice. Our goal is to expand the functionality of the app to make it a viable alternative to the existing GOLD Standard practice app. The app will be built using React Native and Android Studio. Much of the app will be rewritten to remove problematic dependencies present in the previous version of the app. Some expanded functionalities that we plan to implement include a built-in metronome with an algorithm to increase tempo over time, and a compatible Google Calendar itinerary.
